    My name is Blayne and I am a UX Designer, Psychology Major and dabbler in code. I am originally from Kingston, Jamaica, but right now I am living in Toronto, Ontario where I design websites and mobile applications for clients.

    I've worked in various roles in my short time in this world and I've tried many things. I have a BA in Psychology, previously held postions in retail/wholesale, marketing and even entertained the idea of becoming a developer, having dabbled in HTML, CSS, JS, python and building apps using Obj-C. But, none of these feel as natural to me as UX Design, where design and psychology intersect. So, perhaps you need a UX Designer with a background in Psychology? Let's Talk.
